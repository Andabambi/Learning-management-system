Full-Stack Web Development

Course

Full-stack development refers to the method of applying both front-end and back development protocols to develop websites.
This field has been gaining popularity in recent years due to the growing number of digital businesses. It combines the work of managing servers and databases and system engineering. Full-stack developers are in great demand across the world.

THE DURATION OF THE FULL-STACK COURSE

60 hours. All the batches are worked out within a similar time frame. The syllabus has been designed keeping the needs of students in mind.

MODULES (Full-Stack Development Course Syllabus)

HTML is the basic and must have skill-set for every web developer. It is used and extended by various other technologies. To be completely able to understand how things work in web development, you should develop an understanding of HTML. In this section, you will learn

Introduction about HTML: Browsers and HTML; Editor's Offline and Online; Tags, Attribute and Elements; Doctype Element; Comments; Headings, Paragraphs, and Formatting Text Headings, Paragraphs, and Formatting Text; Lists and Links; Images and Tables
