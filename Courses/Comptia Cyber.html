What is Comptia Cybersecurity?

CompTIA Cybersecurity Analyst (CySA+) is an IT workforce certification that applies behavioral analytics to networks and devices to prevent, detect and combat cybersecurity threats through continuous security monitoring.

What is Comptia good for ?

CompTIA Security+ is the first security certification a candidate should earn. It establishes the core knowledge required of any cybersecurity role and provides a springboard to intermediate-level cybersecurity jobs.

Is Comptia good for Cybersecurity

Not only is CompTIA Security+ one of the most widely held and highest paying cybersecurity certifications, it's also a great entry-level, vendor-neutral certification for those new to cybersecurity.
